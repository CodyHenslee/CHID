Multimedia Design 'Coherence Principle' Lesson

Indicators: S1.U, S1.M,S2.C, S2.U, S2.A/E, S3.C, S3.U, S4.CP, S4.L

This Prezi presentation was constructed to provide a brief lesson covering Ruth Colvin Clark and Richard E. Mayer’s ‘Coherence Principle’ found within their guidelines for multimedia learning. The presentation also serves as an example of proper adherence to the coherence principle through the omission of extraneous audio, graphics and words. The lesson utilizes Prezi’s ability to have an instructor determined timeline but also accommodates learners who prefer to be self-paced by allowing them to click around and explore the lesson as they wish.

To construct the presentation, I led a team of collaborators who researched, gathered and created appropriate resources to construct the lesson (S4.CP,S4.L) We decided on Prezi as our solution because it was flexible on the delivery method and provided dynamic multimedia platform to better exemplify the need for such principles as the Clark and Mayer’s ‘Coherence Principle’ and their other guiding principles of multimedia learning (S1.M,S2.C).
