UALR Admissions Received Documents Viewer

Indicators: S1.E, S2.U, S4.RP,S5.Md

The ‘UALR Admissions Received Documents Viewer’ is a prototype solution for easing phone and email traffic to the UALR Office of Admissions by providing an easy, intuitive solution for prospective students to manage and verify the arrival of their required credentials for admissions. The prototype is a web widget that would be added to the student’s application account screen and provides up-to-the-minute access to documents that have been scanned into the their UALR ImageNow document folders. Students also have the ability to report any miss-filed documents within the widget to provide an additional resource for managing and cleaning up student accounts.

The creation of the prototype required the coordination of user testing for evaluation and identifying revisions for the widget (S4.RP). In constructing my study, I had to be ethical in my handling of human test subjects by being forthright and open about their role within the study and how their participation would come to influence the final design (S1.E). The final design adheres to the principles governing human technology interaction by means of its accessibility, ease of use and intuitive design and is supported by further research into other solutions available by mimicking a traditional thumbnail gallery or shadow box that users may come across numerous times, on a daily basis, while exploring the internet (S2.U, S5.Md).
