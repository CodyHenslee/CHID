About Me - Cody Adam Henslee

I’m a soon to be graduate of the University of Arkansas at Little Rock’s Learning Systems Technology Education graduate program and currently hold a Bachelor’s of Art in Art History. My passion for education, combined with my previous experience with photography and design, have led me to a point in my life where I have finally found a rewarding discipline that leverages all of my advanced talents. Instructional Design has allowed me to understand the many principles and theories that have governed much of the education that I have received throughout my life. I am able to reflect on my past experiences in public schools and better understand what mechanisms were in play and how they helped me gain the knowledge and skills that have enabled and shaped me into the person that I am today. This deeper reflection and understanding of the principles and theories that frame our education has enabled me to have become a better instructional designer and educator.

My goal is to respect the hard work of all the teachers who have provided me with a rich education by creating engaging, effective, and technologically enhanced learning environments that will continue to enable another generation of students to excel and obtain the skills necessary for them to make a positive change in our world.
