UALR Admissions 'Double Check' Training

Indicators:S1.U, S2.M,S2.E

The linked website is an office training course developed for the UALR Office of Admissions to assist in the training of student workers for double check. Double check is an important office procedure where student workers verify if documents have been properly filed in ImageNow before the physical copies are shredded.

This course was developed to be accessible to both learners with hearing or vision impairments (S2.E). Because of the nature of the task, accessibility for visual impairment is strictly included for exercise purposes. The course is designed to ensure learners perform within the same context as they will work, with the same resources they will have at their disposal while on the job and to provide flexibility in assessment to accommodate for different types of learners (S1.U,S2.M).
