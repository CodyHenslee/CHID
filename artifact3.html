UALR Admissions Pre-Application Guide

Indicators: S1.C, S1.U, S2.C, S2.U, S2.M, S3.C

Working in the Office of Admissions here at UALR has afforded me the opportunity to solve practical problems through multimedia instruction. The attached storyline module was created in an effort to help ease the anxiety and answer some common questions about applying for admissions to the university.

This UALR pre-application guide utilizes Storyline Articulate to create a web-based solution for providing distance instruction to a diverse population of prospective students (S1.C, S2.C, S3.C). Its design is centered around best practices for multimedia design my limiting extraneous audio, text and images while also ensuring that included audio expands upon what is being presented visually rather than being repetitious (S1.U, S2.U). Learners are allowed to move at their own pace and the information provided within the web module is individualized to withhold extraneous and irrelevant information that does not apply to the specific student’s classification (S2.M).
