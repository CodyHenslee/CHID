Educational Theories Application Papers

Indicators: S2.C, S2.U, S2.M, S5.M

The attached document is a collection of four application papers exploring theories and principles of instruction through their practical applications within a hypothetical office environment. The purpose serves as a means to reflect upon what our community currently knows to be true about the act of learning and how these theories and principles extend beyond academia and apply to a real world context (S2.C,S2.U,S2.M,S5.M).
